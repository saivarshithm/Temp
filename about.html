About Us

About EESA

Get to know the Electrical & Electronics Students Association of NITK Surathkal. We bridge academics and industry through student action.

Our Mission

EESA NITK is a premier student-led organization representing the student body of the Electrical & Electronics Engineering Department at NITK Surathkal. Our main goal is to create a strong network between students, faculty members, and the professional industry.


We host code hackathons, hardware design workshops, guest talks, and social gatherings to promote peer-to-peer learning and active collaboration. We also manage academic databases and library resource repositories to assist students in their coursework and competitive exams.

Vision & Objectives

EESA NITK is governed by core values that aim to foster an atmosphere of growth, technical competence, and community service.

Core Value

Technical Innovation

Promoting hands-on experimentation, circuit building, firmware coding, and AI applications to address real-world electrical grid challenges.

Core Value

Peer Mentorship

Fostering collaboration between seniors and juniors through study groups, exam review sessions, and hardware debugging support.

Core Value

Professional Development

Exposing students to career paths, internship opportunities, research trends, and global alumni connections in electrical sciences.
